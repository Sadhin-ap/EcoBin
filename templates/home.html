Who We Are

Committed to a Greener Tomorrow

EcoBin was founded with a simple mission: make responsible waste disposal effortless for every household and business.

We are a technology-driven waste management company serving residential and commercial clients. Our smart scheduling platform connects you with trained collection teams in minutes.

Every kilogram of waste we collect is sorted, treated and either recycled, composted or safely disposed — ensuring zero landfill impact from our operations.
